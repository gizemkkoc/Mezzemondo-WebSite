Hummus

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt
  • Cumin

Instructions:

  1. Place the cooked chickpeas into a food processor and pulse until they begin to break down.
  2. Add tahini, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, a pinch of salt, and a dash of cumin to the food processor.
  3. Blend thoroughly, stopping occasionally to scrape down the sides to ensure even mixing.
  4. If the hummus is too thick, add a tablespoon of water or olive oil at a time until it reaches your desired creamy consistency.
  5. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed, adding more salt, lemon juice, or cumin to suit your preference.
  6. Transfer to a serving bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and garnish with your favorite toppings such as paprika, parsley, or sesame seeds.
