Baba Ganoush

Ingredients:

  • 3 roasted eggplants
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt
  • Parsley

Instructions:

  1. Peel the roasted eggplants and place them in a large mixing bowl. Mash them with a fork or a potato masher until smooth but slightly chunky for texture.
  2. Mince the garlic and mix it into the mashed eggplants. Add olive oil, a pinch of salt, and finely chopped parsley. Stir thoroughly until all the ingredients are well incorporated and the flavors are evenly distributed.
  3. Transfer the Baba Ganoush to a serving dish. Drizzle with a little extra olive oil on top for added flavor and a glossy finish. Sprinkle with more chopped parsley or your favorite toppings like paprika or pomegranate seeds. Serve with warm pita bread, crackers, or fresh vegetables.
