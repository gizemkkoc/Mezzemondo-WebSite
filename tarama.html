Taramasalata

Ingredients:

  • 200g fish roe
  • 1 slice stale bread
  • 1/2 onion
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Take a slice of stale bread and soak it in water for a few minutes until it softens. Squeeze out the excess water and crumble the bread into small pieces.
  2. In a blender or food processor, combine the fish roe, crumbled bread, and finely chopped onion. Blend until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  3. Gradually add lemon juice to the mixture and continue blending. While blending, slowly drizzle in olive oil, allowing it to emulsify and create a creamy consistency. Add olive oil until the desired texture is reached.
  4. Taste and adjust the flavors, adding more lemon juice for tanginess or a pinch of salt if needed. Serve in a bowl, garnished with a drizzle of olive oil, fresh parsley, or a sprinkle of paprika. Enjoy with warm pita bread, crackers, or fresh vegetables.
