A Fusion of Turkish Tradition & Italian Elegance

In the heart of Florence, we’ve embarked on a journey to bring the vibrant spirit of Turkish mezze culture to life, infused with the warmth of Italian hospitality. What started as a dream inspired by my grandmother’s cherished recipes has flourished into a celebration of flavors that transcend borders.

What sets us apart is our passion for preserving the authentic essence of Turkish mezze while harmonizing it with Italy’s culinary artistry. Every dish we create is a testament to tradition, crafted with love, using locally sourced Italian ingredients and timeless Turkish techniques.

From our cozy shop to your table, we invite you to savor the rich mosaic of flavors that Turkish mezze offers—a culinary journey that unites cultures and brings people together in the most delicious way. Join us, and let’s create unforgettable memories, one mezze at a time.
