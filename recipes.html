Hummus

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, cumin

Instructions:

Process the cooked chickpeas. Add other ingredients and mix again. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle cumin on top.

Baba Ganoush

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggplants
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Half lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, black pepper

Instructions:

Roast and peel the eggplants. Mash and mix with other ingredients. Garnish with pomegranate seeds and parsley.

Tarator

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups walnuts
  • 3 slices stale bread
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions:

Process walnuts and garlic. Add soaked bread crumbs and other ingredients, mix well. Garnish and serve.

Muhammara

Ingredients:

  • 3 red bell peppers
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 2 slices bread
  • Pomegranate molasses
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

Roast and peel the peppers. Process all ingredients. Garnish with walnuts and olive oil.

Taramasalata

Ingredients:

  • 200g fish roe
  • 2 slices stale bread
  • 1 onion
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

Soak the bread crumbs. Mix with fish roe and onion. Add lemon juice and olive oil, mix well.

Eggplant Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggplants
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Parsley
  • Olive oil, lemon

Instructions:

Roast and cube the eggplants. Roast and chop the peppers. Mix all ingredients together and serve.
